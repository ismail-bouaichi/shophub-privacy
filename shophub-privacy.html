Privacy Policy for ShopHub

Last updated: December 18, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to ShopHub. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

ShopHub is a demo/portfolio application. We may collect the following types of information:

  • Location Data: We use Google Maps to provide location-based features such as address selection and delivery tracking. Location data is only accessed when you use these features.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information for app functionality and performance monitoring.
  • Local Storage: The app stores your preferences locally on your device using secure storage.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide location-based services through Google Maps integration
  • Save your app preferences and settings
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

This is a portfolio/demo application that uses static data. Your data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers. We use secure storage mechanisms to protect your information.

5. Third-Party Services

ShopHub integrates with the following third-party service:

  • Google Maps: Used for location services and address selection. Google Maps collects location data according to their privacy policy. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us to have it removed.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or with your explicit consent.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data stored locally on your device
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Disable location permissions in your device settings
